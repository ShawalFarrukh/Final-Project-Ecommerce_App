About The Dry Eye Shop

My story with dry eye

Hi I’m Shawal Farrukh and I’ve been dealing with dry eye for years now. This small shop is a CS50 project inspired by what actually helped me day-to-day to manage my disease: warm compresses, preservative-free drops, lid hygiene, and lifestyle tweaks. I’m not a doctor; I just wanted a simple place to collect and share the basics.

I hope these tools help you build a routine. If you’re struggling, check the Support page for communities and resources that helped me.
